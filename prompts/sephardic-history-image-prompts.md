# Sephardic History — Image Generation Prompts

A series of historically grounded image prompts for AI image generators (Midjourney, DALL-E, Adobe Firefly, etc.), depicting key moments in Sephardic Jewish history.

---

## Prompt 1 — The Capture

> "Roman soldiers binding dark-skinned Hebrew captives in the ruins of Jerusalem, 70 AD, smoke rising from a destroyed stone temple in the background, cinematic oil painting style"

---

## Prompt 2 — Arrival in Sepharad

> "Shackled dark-skinned Hebrew prisoners arriving by Roman ship on the Iberian coast, being greeted by dark-complexioned Jewish merchants already living there, 1st century AD, dramatic golden hour lighting, historical painting style"

---

## Prompt 3 — The Flourishing

> "A prosperous dark-skinned Jewish nobleman in medieval Portugal or Spain, wearing royal robes, seated in a stone courtyard with Hebrew manuscripts and a Portuguese crest on the wall, 12th century, portrait oil painting style"

---

## Prompt 4 — The Reclassification

> "A solemn dark-skinned Sephardic Jewish elder standing before a Spanish royal court, a scribe writing the word 'Negro' in a ledger, the elder's expression dignified but grave, 15th century Iberia, dramatic chiaroscuro lighting"

---
